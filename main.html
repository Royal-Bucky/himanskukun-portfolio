About Me

Hello! I am Himanshu Mishra, a Full Stack Developer passionate about building scalable technologies powered by Generative AI. I am currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering and continuously learning modern software engineering practices, cloud technologies, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence.

Over the past three years, I have designed, developed, and shipped more than 10 software projects ranging from web applications and mobile apps to AI-powered solutions. Throughout this journey, I have worked with technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, React Native, Next.js, Node.js, FastAPI, Python, PostgreSQL, Docker, Git, GitHub, AWS, CI/CD pipelines, REST APIs, and Generative AI.

I am currently working as a Cybersecurity Intern at Sopra Steria, where I am learning enterprise cybersecurity, infrastructure protection, secure environments, network security, identity management, and supporting secure technology solutions for European technology companies.

Alongside my technical journey, I have been serving as the Training & Placement Cell Coordinator for more than two years. During this time, I have managed a team of over 50 members and helped coordinate more than 100 campus recruitment drives, working closely with recruiters, students, and industry professionals.

Previously, I worked as a Software Development Intern at Bytelyst AI, where I contributed to Full Stack Development, Large Language Models (LLMs), Agentic AI systems, and helped build MatchCut, a mobile video editing application.

I also worked as a Full Stack Development Intern at Lakshya for Development, where I revamped the NGO's website, resulting in a 20% reduction in bounce rate and a 50% improvement in page load speed, while enhancing user experience and mobile responsiveness.

Before diving into software engineering, I loved building hardware projects. Some of my favorite projects include a hydraulic robotic arm, a Tesla Coil, and an automatic Rubik's Cube Solver.

Since 2023, I have actively participated in more than 20+ hackathons across Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, and Full Stack Development, constantly learning from real-world problem solving and collaborative development.

Outside programming, I enjoy creating and editing videos, running my gaming YouTube channel, brainstorming startup ideas, and connecting with people from different technical communities.
