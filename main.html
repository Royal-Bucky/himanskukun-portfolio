About Me

I build full-stack applications powered by modern web technologies and Generative AI. Over the past few years, I've developed 10+ production-ready projects, ranging from real-time web platforms and mobile applications to AI-powered developer tools using React, Next.js, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Docker, AWS, and LLMs like GPT-4 and Llama 3.

At Bytelyst AI, I worked on full-stack development and AI integration, contributing to MatchCut, a mobile video editing application with agentic AI features such as automatic caption generation and silent-clip detection. I'm currently a Cybersecurity Intern at Sopra Steria, where I'm gaining hands-on experience in enterprise security, secure infrastructure, and identity management.

Beyond engineering, I serve as the Training & Placement Cell Coordinator at Rajasthan Technical University, leading a team of 50+ members and coordinating 100+ campus recruitment drives while collaborating with recruiters and industry professionals.

My journey into technology started long before software. I loved building hardware projects—from a Hydraulic Robotic Arm and a Tesla Coil to an Automatic Rubik's Cube Solver which sparked my curiosity for solving real-world engineering problems.

Since 2023, I've participated in 20+ hackathons across AI, cybersecurity, blockchain, and full-stack development, constantly learning by building and collaborating with other developers.

Outside of coding, you'll usually find me creating gaming content on YouTube, editing videos, exploring startup ideas, or experimenting with new technologies.
